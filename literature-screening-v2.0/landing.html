工作台核心能力Workspace capabilities

概览工作台在正式筛选项目中的核心能力。Summarizes the workspace capabilities used in formal screening projects.

R

多源记录导入Multi-source import

支持 RIS、NBIB、CSV、BibTeX、ENW、RDF、TXT 等常见格式。Import RIS, NBIB, CSV, BibTeX, ENW, RDF, TXT, and related source exports.

D

去重与规则筛选Deduplication and rules

DOI 精确匹配与标题归一化结合，支持关键词与时间窗口规则。Combine DOI matching, normalized titles, and configurable rule sets across screening stages.

F

全文复核与导出Full-text review and export

支持全文复核、排除理由记录，以及 PRISMA 2020 图表与结果导出。Supports full-text review, exclusion reasons, and publication-facing exports including PRISMA diagrams.

工作流概览Workflow Overview

以五个阶段展示正式筛选与复核流程。Shows the formal screening and review workflow in five stages.

STEP 01

导入记录Import Records

导入多源文件并校验字段。Import source files and validate fields.

STEP 02

筛选标准Screening Criteria

设定纳入、排除、语言和时间条件。Define inclusion, exclusion, language, and year criteria.

STEP 03

初筛结果Screening Summary

执行筛选统计并生成流程图预览。Generate the screening summary and diagram preview.

STEP 04

全文复核Full-Text Review

逐条进行全文复核并记录排除原因。Review full-text decisions and record exclusion reasons.

STEP 05

结果导出Export Deliverables

导出文献清单、流程图与筛选报告。Export study tables, diagrams, and the screening report.
