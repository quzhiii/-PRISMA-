六步工作流Six-Step Workflow

以六个阶段组织完整筛选、质量评价与导出流程。Organizes the full screening, quality, and export process into six stages.

STEP 01

导入记录Import Records

导入多源文献并完成字段识别。Import source files and verify field mapping.

STEP 02

筛选标准Screening Criteria

设置纳入、排除与保护条件。Set inclusion, exclusion, and protection rules.

STEP 03

初筛结果Screening Summary

核对统计结果、去重情况与流程图预览。Review counts, deduplication, and the diagram preview.

STEP 04

全文复核Full-Text Review

完成全文判断并记录排除理由。Review full-text decisions and record exclusion reasons.

STEP 05

质量评价Quality Assessment

建立质量评价队列并确认研究设计、工具族与证据等级基线。Prepare the quality queue and confirm study design, tool family, and evidence baselines.

STEP 06

结果导出Export Deliverables

导出 PRISMA 图、结果表与筛选报告。Export PRISMA diagrams, study tables, and the screening report.
