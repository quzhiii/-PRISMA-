六步审计型工作流Six-step audit-ready workflow

每个阶段都服务于最终可解释的 PRISMA 输出，而不是只追求快速生成图表。Each stage supports explainable PRISMA output rather than just fast diagram generation.

STEP 01

导入与来源记录Import and source trace

导入多源文献，保留来源文件、格式和字段映射信息。Import source files while retaining file, format, and field-mapping context.

STEP 02

保守去重与规则Conservative dedup and rules

硬重复自动移除，疑似重复和筛选规则保留人工复核空间。Auto-remove hard duplicates while keeping candidate duplicates and rules reviewable.

STEP 03

标题摘要筛选Title and abstract screening

生成初筛结果，并把阶段判断写入可导出的 decision ledger。Generate screening results and keep stage decisions in an exportable ledger.

STEP 04

全文复核Full-text review

完成全文判断，记录标准排除理由和复核备注。Review full-text records with standard exclusion reasons and notes.

STEP 05

质量评价Quality appraisal

纳入研究进入质量评价队列，确认研究设计、工具族和证据基线。Move included studies into a quality queue with study design, tool family, and evidence baselines.

STEP 06

PRISMA 与审计包PRISMA and audit package

导出 PRISMA 图、结果表、筛选报告、审计包和双审冲突证据。Export PRISMA diagrams, result tables, screening reports, audit package, and dual-review conflict evidence.
