工作流概览Workflow overview

以六个阶段展示正式筛选、质量评价与审计导出流程。Shows the formal screening, quality appraisal, and audit-export workflow in six stages.

STEP 01

导入记录Import Records

导入多源文件并校验字段。Import source files and validate fields.

STEP 02

保守去重与规则Conservative dedup and rules

设定纳入、排除、保护规则，并保留疑似重复复核空间。Define inclusion, exclusion, and protection rules while preserving candidate duplicate review.

STEP 03

标题摘要筛选Title and abstract screening

执行标题摘要筛选并记录阶段决策。Run title and abstract screening and record stage decisions.

STEP 04

全文复核Full-Text Review

逐条进行全文复核并记录排除原因。Review full-text decisions and record exclusion reasons.

STEP 05

质量评价Quality appraisal

纳入研究进入质量评价队列。Move included studies into the quality appraisal queue.

STEP 06

PRISMA 与审计导出PRISMA and audit export

导出流程图、结果表、报告和审计包。Export diagrams, tables, reports, and the audit package.
